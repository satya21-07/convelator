What is cgpa?

cgpa is cumulative Grade Point Average which is an global educational
grading system. It is used in schools/colleges to measure overall
academic performance of a student. GPA ( Grade Point Average):
It is also a grading average but, it is used to specify only for one term.
And cgpa is an average of all term sgpa.
